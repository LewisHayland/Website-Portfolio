My College life

I attended Farnborough College of Technology, where I studied for three years, taking two different IT courses.

In my first year, I completed a BTEC Level 1/Level 2 course and received a Distinction Distinction as a final grade. In my second year, I compleated a T Level in Digital Production Design and Development as received a Pass as a final grade.



During my T Level Course, I completed 45 days of mandatory work experience at AVI-SPL. During this time, I had the opportunity to work in various departments and gain insight into how each area operates within the company. Here's an overview of my experience:

Design Department: I learned how the team takes sales projects and transforms them into detailed designs for customers. This involved creating lists of required items to complete the projects.

Procurement Department: I observed how the procurement team takes the designs and orders the necessary products, ensuring they are shipped either to the Quality Control Center (QCC) department or directly to customer sites, depending on the product.

Help Desk Department: I gained an understanding of how the help desk manages customer issues, prioritizing cases based on urgency and distributing them among teams. I also learned how they coordinate with international teams to balance workloads.

QCC Department: In the warehouse, I assisted with preparing equipment by installing the latest updates, required programs, and necessary software for company use.

Installation Department: I visited customer sites to help set up offices with the required equipment, ensuring project completion. This included troubleshooting system issues and making necessary adjustments to resolve problems.
